// workflow

From setup to automated
in four steps

WitzFlow is designed for adoption without a change-management project. One admin, one afternoon, live for the semester.

01
🏛️
Institution Registers

The admin registers the institution, sets up departments and academic year, and onboards lecturers with a unique institution code. Under 15 minutes.

02
👨‍🏫
Lecturer Configures Courses

Lecturer creates courses, builds rubrics, and enrolls students via CSV upload or join code. Assignments are scheduled for the semester in one session.

03
🎒
Students Submit & Attend

Students submit work digitally and scan QR codes to mark attendance. Every action is timestamped, tracked, and visible to the lecturer instantly.

04
⚙️
WitzFlow Handles the Rest

AI grades drafts, integrity checks run automatically, weekly reports are generated and sent, and at-risk students surface before the semester ends badly.

// founder_story

"I built WitzClass to digitise assignment collection. I built WitzFlow because the real problem is everything that happens after the submission."

Lecturers in Nigerian institutions manage classes of 200+ students, grade manually, track attendance on paper, and produce reports nobody reads. WitzFlow isn't a feature request — it's a response to a structural problem that wastes thousands of academic hours every semester across Africa.

WitzFlow is built by the same team behind WitzClass, as part of the Wizard Technology product family. The same infrastructure-first philosophy: if it doesn't solve a real problem at a Nigerian institution, it doesn't ship.
